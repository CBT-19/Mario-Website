About Mario

Mario Mario was born on the 9th of July 1981. He is a former plumber who now works as a guardian for Princess Peach and for the people of the Mushroom Kingdom. In his career he has had many successful adventures with people and animals of all kinds. He has had a well known feud with King Koopa, A.K.A. Bowser, who has been known to kidnap the princess and send the Mushroom Kingdom into turmoil. Fortunately, Mario Has been there to help the people of the Mushroom Kingdom in order to stop Bowser, and save the princess. His story has become so well known that directors have recently asked Mario to be featured in a movie about his life. Said movie is currently in production.
